## [[Aortic Stenosis]]

Senile calcification is the commonest cause - think AS in any elderly patient with triad of: 

1. [[Angina]]
2. Syncope
3. [[Heart failure]]


## Aortic sclerosis

Senile degeneration of the valve. Ejection systolic murmur but no carotid radiation and normal pulse.
